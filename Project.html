Life with Covid

My days in quarantine has been the most boring event to ever occur in the history of my life. This is my first pandemic to ever experience and its nothing compared to those zombie apocalypse on tv.

I would had loved to do so many things before this worldwide quarantine shutdown began, but unfortunately on the very last weekend before the city shutdown, I got sick.

So now I spend most of my time inside my room playing nonstop video games, tv, eating, cleaning, and online classes. The only thing that keeps me busy is school work and online meetings at work.

So my birthday happened during quarantine and since I couldn’t go out and celebrate, I did a little project and invited my close family to celebrate. The best part was my sister making a delicious strawberry shortcake from scratch and it was amazing also my favorite.

I obviously get bored and can’t help but to go out grocery shopping, of course I take precautionary remedies and wear my face mask.

It has been nearly 2 months since the citywide shutdown and people are desperate to go back to their normal lives. I though by the summer things would go back to the way they were but they already cancelled it.

I am hoping they find a cure to this soon so I can go back and enjoy life with no regrets.
